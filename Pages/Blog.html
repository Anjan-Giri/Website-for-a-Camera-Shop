About Technology

Technology is the process of improving products, opinions, infrastructure by using scientific knowledge and ideas. It makes our lifestyle very smoothl, accurate, fast and efficiency. With the help of modern technology, we can communicate with each other from any corner of the world without any issue. Social media like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Youtube etc. we can listen news, events, films of any places of the world. Even in the medical sector, technology can help to treat sick people by using different types of gadgets and devices. As such, it is important to use technology with more responsibly and thoughtfully, with an eye towards maxmizing its positive impact on society.

Positive Impact

At first, the evolution of technology is very beneficial for human life in several ways. In different sector like medical, business, school, industry, company technology has great positive impact. Some of the positive impact of technology are listed below:

  1. It boosts productivity and efficiency of human activities.
  2. It allows us to perform tasks in less time.
  3. We can get information of any corner of the world by using smartphones.
  4. It helps to treat sick people from several diseases with the help of modern gadgets.
  5. Technology turns our complex tasks into interesting and simple.

Negative Impact

As we know, Technology helps to improve our lifestyle in several ways and makes our life simple and easy. Beside all the merits, there are also some demerits of technology. Some of the negative impact of technology are listed below:

  1. Technology can lead to social isolation as many people spend more time.
  2. It can harm our physical and mental health.
  3. It hampers on relatioships and communication skills.
  4. It can replace jobs as machines and automation becomes more advanced.
  5. It can affect our privacy and our personal information
